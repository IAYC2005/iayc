About INK All-You-Can (IAYC)

Since 2005, INK All-You-Can (IAYC) has been recognized as Metro Manila’s leading ink refilling station and a trusted provider of alternative printer consumables. We are committed to delivering reliable, high-quality solutions through advanced remanufacturing and refilling technologies for both inkjet and laser printers.

Commitment to Quality
Our dedication to excellence is reflected in our use of brand-specific, high-grade inks powered by Ink Precision Technology (IPT). We also utilize custom-engineered tools and equipment sourced from ISO 9000-certified manufacturers, ensuring optimal performance, safety, and long-term durability for your printing equipment.

Trusted by Multiple Industries
We continuously improve our products and services to maintain our leadership in the industry. Today, we proudly serve a wide range of sectors including telecommunications, real estate, banking, healthcare, and many more.
