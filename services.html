SERVICES


1. Ink Refilling Services
Enjoy high-quality and affordable printing through our professional ink refilling services. We use brand-specific, high-grade inks powered by Ink Precision Technology (IPT) to deliver vibrant colors, sharp text, and consistent printing results—while helping extend the lifespan of your printer.

2. Brand-New Compatible Toner Cartridges (For Laser Printers)
We offer brand-new, high-quality compatible toner cartridges designed for reliable performance and excellent print output. All units are tested for compatibility, durability, and efficiency—providing a cost-effective alternative to OEM toner without compromising quality.

3. Printer Repair & Maintenance
Our skilled technicians provide quick and dependable repair and maintenance services for both inkjet and laser printers. From diagnostics and cleaning to mechanical adjustments and part replacements, we ensure your device operates smoothly and efficiently.

4. Continuous Ink Supply System (CISS) Installation
Optimize your printing productivity with our professional CISS setup. Ideal for high-volume printing, our installation ensures stable ink flow, reduced printing costs, and consistent output—perfect for businesses, schools, and homes.

5. Printer Parts Replacement
We provide high-quality replacement parts such as printheads, rollers, belts, sensors, and maintenance kits. All parts are sourced from trusted suppliers to ensure reliability and long-term performance.

6. Diagnostic & Isolation Services
Unsure what’s wrong with your printer? Our diagnostic service identifies issues using professional-grade tools. After assessment, we provide a clear explanation and recommend the most practical and affordable solution.
